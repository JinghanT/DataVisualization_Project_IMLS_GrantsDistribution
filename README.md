    <article class="markdown-body entry-content" itemprop="text"><h1><a id="user-content-final-project-lis590dv---spring-2017" class="anchor" href="#final-project-lis590dv---spring-2017" aria-hidden="true"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path fill-rule="evenodd" d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z"></path></svg></a>Final Project: LIS590DV - Spring 2017</h1>
<p>Your final project is composed of four parts.  You will work in groups of three
or four people; each person will be expected to participate in the project.</p>
<p>Note that in some cases, the descriptions of <em>what</em> you are to visualize are
somewhat vague.  This is in keeping with our discussion of carefully choosing
the story you want to tell.  You're going to be called upon in this assignment
to examine the data, and then within the rough outlines of the discussion,
construct a visualization that "answers" the posed question.</p>
<p>There are a few important notes:</p>
<ul>
<li>All of the source code must be provided.</li>
<li>Use GitHub to version your code and your writeup.  This will also be used to
verify individual contributions.</li>
<li>The writeup will be evaluated.</li>
<li>A "visualization" does not mean a single plot; it is broadly defined to
include linked visualizations, collections of plots, etc.</li>
</ul>
<h2><a id="user-content-component-1-procedural-bespoke-visualization" class="anchor" href="#component-1-procedural-bespoke-visualization" aria-hidden="true"><svg aria-hidden="true" class="octicon octicon-link" height="16" version="1.1" viewBox="0 0 16 16" width="16"><path fill-rule="evenodd" d="M4 9h1v1H4c-1.5 0-3-1.69-3-3.5S2.55 3 4 3h4c1.45 0 3 1.69 3 3.5 0 1.41-.91 2.72-2 3.25V8.59c.58-.45 1-1.27 1-2.09C10 5.22 8.98 4 8 4H4c-.98 0-2 1.22-2 2.5S3 9 4 9zm9-3h-1v1h1c1 0 2 1.22 2 2.5S13.98 12 13 12H9c-.98 0-2-1.22-2-2.5 0-.83.42-1.64 1-2.09V6.25c-1.09.53-2 1.84-2 3.25C6 11.31 7.55 13 9 13h4c1.45 0 3-1.69 3-3.5S14.5 6 13 6z"></path></svg></a>Component 1: Procedural, Bespoke Visualization</h2>
<p><strong>Concept</strong>: Tell us about the routes in the Champaign-Urbana Metro Transit
District.</p>
<p>Your first dataset will be based on the <a href="http://cutmd.com/">CUMTD</a> data.  There

<p>Your visualization should be constructed in a procedural way, and should be
